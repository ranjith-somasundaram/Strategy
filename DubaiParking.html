A quick guide

How to use

  1. Tap your name, then your action. Pick yourself each time — nobody stays selected.
  2. Not coming in? Tap "I'm not coming today." Your bay turns orange and opens up for anyone.
  3. Need a bay? Tap any glowing orange stall to grab it. It's yours for the day.
  4. Plans changed?
    • Freed your bay, nobody took it yet — tap "take it back."
    • Someone already grabbed it — it's theirs today; message them in the chat if you really need it.
    • Grabbed one you won't use — tap "free it up" for the next person.

Keep it fair

  • No login here — the app trusts everyone to play straight.
  • Tap only your own name. You can act as anyone; please don't.
  • Free your bay only if you're truly not coming.
  • Grab a bay only if you'll use it — and free it up if plans change.
  • First come, first served. If a stall isn't glowing, it's taken.

Good to know

  • An orange glow means free. Dim means taken or in use.
  • Changes show up for everyone within a few seconds.
  • Tuesday and Wednesday are separate — switch the day at the top.
